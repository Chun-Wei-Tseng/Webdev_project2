Alice's at Skyline

The Top Car Enthusiast Destination in the Bay

Nestled in the moutains that seperate the valley from the coast, Alice's restaurant is the most popular and most known spot for car enthusiasts and motorcycle riders to stop, meet and converse while they people watch and car spot. At any given day are are bound to see tens or even up to a hundred spots at this location.

BMW Motorsport House

Close to Our Hearts

Right before Skyline Boulevard dumps you off onto Alice's restaurant, the BMW Motorsport is tucked away in a small corridor. You will miss it the first few times as it is on the side of the road and is easy to fly by as you are winding through those turns. Be on the lookout for a black flag with a BMW Motorsport logo perched right off of the side of the road.

Crystal Springs Resevoir

Back to Normality

As you drive right through the iconic Skyline Boulevard all the way down to Crystal Springs Resevoir, this is the last stop before you hop back on Interstate 280 and head back to normality. The pulloff on the side of the road is a popular meeting spot for enthusiasts to get a final chat in before they hang up their driver's gloves for the week and head back to work.

Vista Point

Expansive Views of the Entire Valley

Temporarily closed during the Post-Covid world, this small pull off houses about 20 parking spots perched right off a steep embankment with breathtaking panoramic views.
