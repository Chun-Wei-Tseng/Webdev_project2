Los Gatos

"Here, there are no other things, just you and the car." - R.Wang

Cathermola Road

Tight Bend Passion:

This road is not wide as a highway, but the driver of this F87 M2 CS indeed had a lot of fun driving on it. All the tight bends translate into sheer driving pleasure, and even better, you can stop anytime to enjoy the fantastic road.

Loma Prieta

Chill after the Uphill Endeavor:

At the end of the road, this parking lot will emerge; it is a spot that can accommodate up to 10 cars. This place is where drivers gather after the run, after their morning blast the owners parked this Honda Red S2000 and Grey NC Miata in this lot. Please note, this place is at a high elevation; you might need to pack your winter jacket before you head out.

Mount Umunhum Trail Head

The Gathering Place:

Instead of heading to Loma Prieta, heading the opposite way will lead you to this location. The parking structure sits in the shadow of "The Cube", a Cold War Era radar tower used to intercept and watch for hostile aircraft. Nowadays, the only foreign thing you will find are import vehicles, from Japanese hot hatches to Porsche 911s, as a gearhead, just standing here, meeting new friends, and seeing cars should be enough entertainment for a whole day.

Lake Elsman

Enjoy the Warm Breeze of the Engine

Down the Hill, cars like this E87 BMW Z4 always stop by the lake. Join them for a moment of peace and carry on. This location is also a great spot if you are spending just an hour or so in Los Gatos because it is only fifteen-minute short drive from downtown Los Gatos.
