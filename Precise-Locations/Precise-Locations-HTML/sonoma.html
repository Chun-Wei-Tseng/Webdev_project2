Get Your Engines Ready

A heavily modified Porsche 911 track car agressively takes the corner and proceeds to gas all the way through turn 1. Next stop, the chute.

A Quick Breather Before the Turns

It's not too often that you see a Mclaren F1, let alone a Mclaren F1 GTR, and to see this marvelous machine being driven at the limit is a sight to behold. This is a 30 million dollar car by the way, imagine using that as your track day beater!

Turn 8

Sidewinder Territory

The huge rear wing on that BMW Sauber F1 car generates downforce to glue the car's rear end into the hot asphault and help those sticky rubber tires hook up. That is the only way you can take that turn at 150 miles per hour.

Turn 11

The Most Technical Part of the Track

Gas through the straightaway only to SLAM on the brakes in order to take turn 11. This turn will come up fast on you and if you are not prepared you will understeer straight into the pit.
