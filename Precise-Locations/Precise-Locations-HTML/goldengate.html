Fort Point

The Historic Military Installation

Built in the 1800s, Fort Point was once the first line of defense for military invaders hailing from the Pacific. Nowadays, you can head over to this spot to catch some iconic views in the backdrop.

Golden Gate Bridge

The Crown Jewel of the Bay Area

Millions of tourists and locals flock to this iconic landmark every year, but it is a frequent spot for car rallies and events as well. There is no greater feeling that cruising in a caravan of 50 cars in the warm California sun as you cross over the Bay.
