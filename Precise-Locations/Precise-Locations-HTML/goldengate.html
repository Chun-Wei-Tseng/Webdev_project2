AutoMall Fremont

"There is more to it than shopping for a new car." - R.Wang

End of the AutoMall Parkway

Urban Green Isle:

At the end of the auto mall parkway, there is an almost mile-long straight where gearheads can practice riding their sport bikes and launching their manual cars. It is a great place to spot some rare vehicles in the backdrop of busy downtown Fremont, like this E39 M5 and E60 M5 gathering to enjoy the moment of peace.

San Francisco Bay National Wildlife Reserve

Cars and Nature

Here, you could see campers, hikers, bikers, and gearheads. There are many wetland and dirt roads here, making it a heaven for Rally Car Fans and off-roaders. Rally legends like WRX STI and Lancer Evolution are reunited here.

Fremont Bart Station

Mini Tokyo:

This Station Parking lot is busy and packed with people in the daylight, but in the night, it becomes a gathering place for gearheads. Specifically for Japanese car lovers - the BART trail behind make it feel like you are right at Tokyo JR station. People gather here and take pictures as if they are some 8000 miles away.

Palomares Road

Sunday Slow Cruise

Behind busy Downtown Fremont, there is a pretty back road link, some quiet ranches with a few fantastic wineries. This road tends to be very busy, especially on Sunday Mornings, when gearheads want to warm up their engine not too far from home. Wou will be pleasantly surprised by the movement, like this F80 M3 on the road.
