Santana Row

"A gathering place for car geeks." - R.Wang

Santana Row Main Street

Santana Row Main Street:

For exotic vehicle lovers, Santana Row becomes heaven for car spots on the fourth Sunday of every month. The Cars and Croissants monthly event is regularly held here. Only exotics are allowed here, so pack up your camera bag and head over here. You won't be disappointed.

Tesla Santana Row

Experience Tesla

One of only a few Tesla experience stores in the Bay Area. Here you can enjoy a demo of the latest technology from Bay Area native auto builder, and you could place your direct order from this location as well. Not a bad place to kill some time.

American Motors Custom and Classics

Travel Back in Time

Despite the sign out front showcasing a Toyota used cars logo, that sign is as far as what is actually inside. Stored inside are lots of classic cars, almost exclusively American Muscle Era cars and trucks. Sometimes they are in a little bit of rougher condition, but they are always ready to drive and usually at a super reasonable price. Step back to the 60's when you step into this shop.

Porsche Stevens Creek

One of the Most Exotic Dealerships in Silicon Valley

Probably the most prestigious dealership around Stevens Creek, when you visit this dealer there is good chance you will see plenty of "made-to-order" Porsches here. Sometimes you even run into something extraordinary like a Porsche 918 Spyder or Carrera GT. There is even a litte Porsche Gift Shop in here as well; you could come here and grab an excellent gift for your friends.
