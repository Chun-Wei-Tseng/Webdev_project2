California Highway Nine

"Always a great spot to see great variety of vehicles." - R.Wang

Saratoga Ave

Racing to the Top:

Wind through the most iconic hairpin turns on California Highway Nine - this Triple Yellow Ford Mustang Shelby GT350 is fighting a speed battle against the FK8 Honda Civic Type R behind it. This section of Highway 9 crowns a victor at the top not based on whomever has the bigger engine, but rather the technical skill and ability of the driver.

Santa Cruz Mountain Vista Point

Where bonds are formed:

After the joy of the chase up the mountain, stop at this popular pulloff, get out, and gaze at the beautiful Santa Cruz Mountain Range. Here you can catch up with old friends, meet new enthusiasts, finally get to that warm cup of morning coffee... The friendships formed here are unbreakable.

Big Basin Road

Ride Down with Gravity:

Instead of powering through them, the corners on Big Basin Road are the most challenging & fun part of this ambitious endeavor; this Porsche 991 GT3 and Toyota A90 Supra make their way down the hill. A sight from Heaven.

Seeds of Knowledge, Preschool

Cool the Engine and Do It Again:

On weekdays, this is a spot for young knowledge seekers; over the weekend, a perfect spot for all gearheads. First, we gather and rewind about our fun ride, pay tribute to our cars, and talk about excellent motorsport rivalries like the CZ4a Lancer Evolution and VAB WRX STI. Then, after the brakes are cooled, we go up again.
