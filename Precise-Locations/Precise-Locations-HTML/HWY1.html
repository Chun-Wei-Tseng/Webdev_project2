California Highway One

"A captivating cruise across the California coast." - E.Khouri

Bixby Bridge

Landslide Territory

Take a drive down the beatiful California coast, enjoy the cool ocean breeze and park off on the side if you want to walk the length of the bridge. Just don't come down here during rainy season, it's landslide territory...

Pacifica State Beach

A Surfer's Paradise

A cranking spot renowned by surfers all over, be sure to visit this beach after you drive through the Tom Lantos Tunnel in Pacifica. This popular spot is usually the starting point for rallies headed down the California Coast on highway 1.

Pelican Vista Point

A Windy Pulloff Before the Boardwalk

About an hour down the coast from Pacifica, this popular pulloff is a popular rest stop for car enthusiasts and adventurers alike. After jumping back into your car and getting back on the road, the next stop after this is Santa Cruz.

Tom Lantos Tunnels

Bring Some Earplugs

You will hear them before you see them; the loud, high pitched scream of sport bikes, the deep growl of big displacment V8s, every single audible sound reverbing off the walls a thousand times over. This should be your first stop after you slap a modified exhaust system on your car.
