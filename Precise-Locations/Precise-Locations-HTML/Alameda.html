Alameda

"Abandoned U.S. Navy base, heaven for gearheads." - R.Wang

RWY 13-31

The Main Street for Bay Area Car Culture:

Used to be where fighter jets took off and landed; the "need for speed" is still here, but with fast cars. This is the only place a car enthusiast can floor their vehicle and run flat out. So naturally, it became holy ground for all car guys. Fast cars and enthusiasts only!

Hanger Area

Cars and Beers

All the old hangers here have transformed into modern bars best suited for a night out on the weekend. So naturally it became a place where you could see exotic sports cars like this Porsche 718 Coupe. Settle down and grab a beer while the good cars and good times last.

RWY 7-25

San Francisco Skyline

This might be the best place to spot the San Francisco Skyline on a clear day. It is an excellent spot to see some cool cars moving around with SF skyline in the background. Grab your long shot lens and expect some good action to show.

USS Hornet

Big Toys vs. Small Toys

Once a trademark of America's might, now a museum floating on the sea. We all love the toys - big ancient toys like classic cars and aircraft carriers. Seeing this Honda NSX in front of the USS hornet is extraordinary to all.
